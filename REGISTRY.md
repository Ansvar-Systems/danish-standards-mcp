# Registry Descriptions

## npm

Danish government cybersecurity standards MCP -- CFCS, Digitaliseringsstyrelsen, Statens ISO 27001, D-maerket, Datatilsynet, Sundhedsdatastyrelsen. Part of the Ansvar MCP Network (ansvar.ai/mcp).

## Smithery

Access Danish government cybersecurity standards including CFCS guidance, Digitaliseringsstyrelsen security requirements, Statens ISO 27001, D-maerket digital trust label, Datatilsynet measures, and SDS healthcare IT security. Bilingual (Danish + English) with FTS search, ISO 27001/27002 cross-references, and sector-based filtering.

## Glama

Danish government cybersecurity standards MCP covering 6 frameworks and 227+ controls. Search by keyword, sector, or ISO mapping. Bilingual Danish/English support.
